About Me

About Me

Workplace Writing Experience

At my job as a software developer, I write technical documentation that explains the custom solution we have created for our clients with detailed instructions on how they can interact with it. Outside of technical documentation, I form status updates daily that provide my managers with insight into what I have worked on, what my plan is for the rest of the day, and any blockers/concerns that I might have.

Relationship with Workplace Writing

My relationship with writing has evolved throughout the years. I now understand the importance of good technical documentation and how having a central location of information saves a lot of back and forth in the workplace. When I first started out, I didn’t quite comprehend the need for formal updates and having a trail for every conversation but working remotely for the past 2 has changed my mindset.

Goals

My goals for this course are to be able to communicate my opinions or any issues that I’m facing in a clear and concise manner.
